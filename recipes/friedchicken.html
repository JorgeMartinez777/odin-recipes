Description

Fried Chicken is a hot and steamy fried chicken leg or breast dipped in sauce and fried up to perfection.

Ingredients

  • Chicken leg or breasts
  • Flour
  • Bowl of flavoring
  • Deep Fryer

Steps

  1. Mix chicken legs and/or breasts in flour.
  2. Mix those same chicken legs and/or breasts in the bowl of flavoring.
  3. Dip the chicken in the fryer and wait until it's a golden color ,BE CAREFUL it's hot, and enjoy!
