Mac N Cheese

Go back to main page

Description

Mac N Cheese is a creamy, gooey, and delicious dish that contains macaroni covered in warm cheese, usually served in a bowl.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni of your choice
  • Cheese of your choice
  • Water

Steps

  1. Mix macaroni, cheese, and water in a microwave safe bowl.
  2. Place the bowl in a mircowave and set it for 2 minutes.
  3. Take out bowl,BE CAREFUL it's hot, and enjoy!
